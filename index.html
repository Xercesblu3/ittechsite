Business Success Framework

90-Day Plan of Action for Business Growth

A practical roadmap business leaders can execute to improve operations, increase revenue, and build momentum in the next 90 days.

How to Use This Plan

This framework is written for business owners, operators, and leadership teams. Assign an owner for each action item, schedule weekly check-ins, and track outcomes every 30 days.

Days 1-30: Build the Foundation

  • Define one measurable 90-day business goal.
  • Audit your sales, operations, and customer journey.
  • Create a weekly KPI dashboard (leads, close rate, margin).
  • Document your top 5 repeatable processes.

Days 31-60: Execute and Optimize

  • Launch one focused marketing and sales campaign.
  • Improve conversion steps in your sales pipeline.
  • Implement one cost-saving or efficiency initiative.
  • Coach your team on accountability and reporting cadence.

Days 61-90: Scale What Works

  • Double down on top-performing channels and offers.
  • Set a hiring or delegation plan for critical bottlenecks.
  • Build a 6-month forecast from current performance data.
  • Finalize your next 90-day execution roadmap.

Weekly Leadership Checklist

  1. Review KPI trends and identify blockers.
  2. Confirm owners and deadlines for each priority.
  3. Address client/customer feedback and retention actions.
  4. Adjust strategy based on real results, not assumptions.

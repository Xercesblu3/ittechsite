Client-Ready Plan of Action

90-Day Business Execution Plan

Give this plan directly to leadership teams as a practical roadmap for growth, operational control, and measurable business outcomes.

How Businesses Should Run This Plan

Set one business objective for the 90-day period (revenue, margin, retention, or pipeline growth). Assign one accountable owner per action, hold weekly reviews, and report progress every 30 days.

Days 1-30: Diagnose + Prioritize

  • Define baseline KPIs and the 90-day target.
  • Audit lead flow, sales conversion, and service delivery.
  • Identify top 3 growth bottlenecks impacting profit.
  • Approve owners, budget, and weekly reporting format.

Deliverable: 30-day scorecard and priority map.

Days 31-60: Implement + Improve

  • Launch one targeted campaign to increase qualified demand.
  • Standardize sales follow-up and proposal turnaround time.
  • Reduce delivery friction with process and staffing updates.
  • Track weekly variance against KPI goals and intervene quickly.

Deliverable: KPI trend report with mid-course adjustments.

Days 61-90: Scale + Formalize

  • Scale the highest-converting channel and best offer.
  • Lock in SOPs for sales, fulfillment, and client success.
  • Build a 6-month operating forecast from actual results.
  • Publish the next 90-day plan with clear capacity targets.

Deliverable: Growth playbook and next-quarter roadmap.

Weekly Business Review Agenda

  1. KPI review: leads, conversion, revenue, margin, retention.
  2. Priority review: progress by owner, by due date, by risk.
  3. Customer review: feedback, churn threats, upsell opportunities.
  4. Decision review: what to stop, start, and scale this week.
